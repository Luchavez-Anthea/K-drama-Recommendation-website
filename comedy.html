Comedy K-Dramas

Brighten your day with hilarious Korean dramas filled with witty humor, lovable characters, and unforgettable moments. Whether you enjoy romantic comedies, quirky friendships, or laugh-out-loud adventures, these K-dramas are guaranteed to keep you smiling from beginning to end.

Fight For My Way

Year Released: 2017

Audience Rating: ⭐ 8.6/10

My Rating: ⭐ 9.8/10

Four lifelong friends chase their dreams while balancing careers, friendship, and unexpected romance.

Why I Recommend It: It feels so real and relatable. The chemistry between the characters is amazing, and you'll laugh just as much as you'll cheer for their dreams.

Business Proposal

Year Released: 2022

Audience Rating: ⭐ 8.7/10

My Rating: ⭐ 10/10

A woman pretends to be her friend on a blind date, only to discover her date is her company's CEO.

Why I Recommend It: This is one of the funniest romantic comedies I've watched. The comedy never feels forced, and the romance will keep you smiling every episode.

Welcome to Waikiki

Year Released: 2018

Audience Rating: ⭐ 8.9/10

My Rating: ⭐ 10/10

Three friends running a struggling guesthouse find their lives turned upside down when a single mother and her baby move in.

Why I Recommend It: If you need a good laugh, this is the perfect drama. Every episode is packed with hilarious moments that never get old.

Mr.Queen

Year Released: 2020

Audience Rating: ⭐ 9.0/10

My Rating: ⭐ 10/10

A modern-day chef's soul is transported into the body of a Joseon queen, leading to hilarious and unexpected situations.

Why I Recommend It: It's one of the funniest K-dramas ever made. The comedy, romance, and historical setting blend together perfectly.

The Trauma Code: Heroes on Call

Year Released: 2025

Audience Rating: ⭐ 8.8/10

My Rating: ⭐ 9.5/10

A brilliant trauma surgeon joins a struggling emergency team, bringing both life-saving skills and unexpected humor to the hospital.

Why I Recommend It: It balances exciting medical cases with lighthearted comedy, making it fun to watch without losing its emotional impact.
