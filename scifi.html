Sci-Fi K-Dramas

Discover mind-bending Korean dramas filled with superpowers, futuristic worlds, time-bending adventures, and unforgettable stories.

Moving

Year Released: 2023

Audience Rating: ⭐ 9.1/10

My Rating: ⭐ 10/10

Teenagers with extraordinary abilities try to live normal lives while their parents do everything they can to protect them from dangerous organizations.

Why I Recommend It: I expected it to be all about superpowers, but what really stayed with me was the love between the families. The action is amazing, but the emotional moments made me care about every character.

W: Two Worlds

Year Released: 2016

Audience Rating: ⭐ 8.7/10

My Rating: ⭐ 9.7/10

A young doctor is suddenly pulled into a webtoon where she meets its heroic main character, blurring the line between fiction and reality.

Why I Recommend It: This drama kept surprising me with every episode. I loved trying to figure out what would happen next, and the romance made the story even more unforgettable.

The Atypical Family

Year Released: 2024

Audience Rating: ⭐ 8.3/10

My Rating: ⭐ 9.5/10

A family with supernatural abilities begins losing their powers until a mysterious woman enters their lives and changes everything.

Why I Recommend It: I enjoyed how it wasn't just about superpowers. It tells a heartfelt story about healing, family, and second chances, making it both exciting and emotional.

Black Knight

Year Released: 2023

Audience Rating: ⭐ 7.8/10

My Rating: ⭐ 9.3/10

In a future where clean air is scarce, a legendary delivery driver risks everything to protect refugees and fight against an oppressive society.

Why I Recommend It: The futuristic world immediately caught my attention, but what I loved most was the determination of the characters. It made me appreciate how hope can still exist even in the darkest situations.
